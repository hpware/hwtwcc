Why is this website self hosted?

UPDATE: THIS WEBSITE DUE TO DOWNTIME TO THE ROUTER, ITS NO LONGER SELF HOSTED, THIS POST CONTAINS INCORRECT INFORMATION.

If you don't know, this website is hosted using IIS because my hardware is not capable of running Debian. I use Vercel to host another website (https://hpware.hwtw.cc). This website is light and won't take much resources that Vercel limits.

You might ask: "But no Vercel?"
Well, first I would like to put all images and videos on the same place where the website is hosted – which is this one.
Second, Vercel is not free; after 100GB of bandwidth, you can't use it for free. I have to pay for it.

Well, how about Cloudflare? It offers:
1. Unlimited bandwidth
2. A great free tier
3. Fits my domains' NS

Bad things?
1. Cloudflare's bad take on "sales" and banning their accounts – I don't like downtime.

And that's about it. I will maybe move this website elsewhere; I guess that makes VPS more suitable for this application.

That's the end of my ranting. Thanks for reading!
